CRITICAL FIX: client.dll and particles.dll Crashes
I've fixed those annoying crashes that kept happening exactly when someone died. 

What was happening:
The game was crashing randomly for some of you when enemies died, especially if you had "Kill Ghost Chams" turned on.
Basically, the cheese was trying to force the game to draw the dead bodies twice to create that cool ghost effect.
But the game engine didn't like how I was doing it, and it was getting confused trying to clean up the dead bodies' particles and animations, which caused the crashes.

What I fixed:
I completely removed the old method of forcing those ghost chams on dead bodies.
Now, I just let the game handle dead bodies exactly how it's supposed to naturally.

What this means for you:
No more random game crashes or freezes when you kill someone!
I had to temporarily remove the "Kill Ghost Chams" effect on dead bodies to ensure your game stays 100% stable during intense fights.
